Rock, Paper, Scissors, Shoot!

Personal Project • Designer • Programmer • Unity

Project Overview

Role Designer & Programmer
Team Size 4 people
Timeframe December 2022
(3 weeks)
Engine Unity

In this top-down bullet hell, players must master the power of rock, paper, and scissors to reclaim their school. Three gangs of 5th graders have taken over — The Rock Resistance, the Paper Platoon, and the Scissor Squad. Players and Billy, the only brave 2nd graders willing to fight back, must master each weapon type to defeat each gang's leader.

The game was built in 3 weeks as a class final project. I was one of two members responsible for both programming and design. The core challenge: make each weapon type feel necessary by designing encounters where ignoring any one of them dramatically increases difficulty — just like a real game of RPS.

Design Goals

The goal was a silly but mechanically honest bullet hell where cooldown management across three weapon types creates genuine strategic decisions. Each boss should be noticeably easier when engaged with the correct counter-weapon, giving players a clear incentive to learn and rotate their arsenal rather than relying on one approach.

My Contributions

  • Prototyped and implemented player movement, 3 basic enemy types, 1 boss, and 2 obstacle types
  • Designed the weapon cooldown system to ensure all three weapon types felt useful
  • Built the weapon and health bar UI
  • Implemented weapon sound effects and background music
