Unchained: Tale of Enra

August 2024 - Present

Role: Lead Designer

Unchained: Tale of Enra is a tactical RPG that reimagines traditional turn-based combat by introducing a unique peace system. Players can choose to either defeat their enemies in traditional tactical combat or attempt to make peace with them through careful positioning and dialogue choices. Each approach offers different rewards and impacts the story's progression, creating a rich, branching narrative experience.

Contributions

  • Led the design team in developing and implementing the core peace-making mechanics

  • Created and balanced the tactical combat system, including unit stats, abilities, and movement rules

  • Designed the dialogue system that integrates with the peace-making mechanics

  • Worked closely with the narrative team to ensure gameplay mechanics supported the story themes

  • Developed and maintained the game design document (GDD)

  • Conducted regular playtesting sessions and implemented feedback-driven improvements
