Unchained: Tale of Enra

August 2024 - Present

Role: Designer

Unchained: Tale of Enra is a tactical RPG that reimagines traditional turn-based combat by introducing a unique 'Negotiation' system. Players can choose to either defeat their enemies using traditional tactical combat AND/OR by convincing them to lay down their arms via breaking their Resolve with non-violent means. Each approach offers different rewards and different methods of success, impacting the protagonists' development and choices in the narrative.

This game was originally created as a Capstone project in the USC Games program using Unity, and has since expanded beyond that to further explore its potential after winning awards at the 2025 USC Games Expo. It is currently still in development.

Contributions

Negotiation System

A Core mechanic that changes the genre

The Negotiation system is a core mechanic designed by me with help from the rest of design that differentiates Unchained from traditional tactical RPGs. Players can choose to either defeat enemies in combat or convince them to lay down their arms, offering multiple approaches to each encounter.

In this system, players can spend their action-economy on a turn by Negotiating. In this system, players select pre-made responses as they converse with enemies in an attempt to reduce their Resolve. The Resolve meter is present on all enemies throughout a level and affects their behavior with player characters depending on its value. Enemies may become debuffed, act more passively, flee, or even join the player's side if the right conditions are met.

Throughout development, I worked with designers to iterate on the system. It led to many prototypes and different versions of the UI. Extensive playtesting in early development was essential in refining the system.

Tactical Combat System

Turn-based tactical gameplay

The tactical combat system features turn-based grid-based movement, unit positioning, and ability usage. Each unit has unique abilities and stat distributions that create strategic depth and encourage multiple playstyles.

Dialogue Integration

Dialogue choices impact gameplay outcomes

Our dialogue system seamlessly integrates with the Negotiation mechanics, allowing player choices and conversations to directly impact combat scenarios and enemy decisions. This creates meaningful moments where words matter as much as actions.

Design Philosophy

Player agency and meaningful choices

We designed Unchained around the philosophy of giving players agency and meaningful choices. Every encounter can be approached in multiple ways, and the story responds to the player's decisions, rewarding both combat mastery and negotiation skill.
